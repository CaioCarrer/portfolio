Visual Identity for Youtube

The project was developed for the Project 2 course within the Graphic Design program at Unesp Bauru. It consisted of creating the visual identity for a YouTube channel and a Twitter page.
At the time I did this project I was fascinated by the Vaporwave aesthetic and its associated musical style. With the freedom to choose the theme for this visual identity, I saw an opportunity to apply this style that I admired so much.

The main characteristics of this style are vibrant colors and gradients. The chosen colors were purple (#230025), yellow (#E9C42A), pink (#F8005B), and orange for the duck's beak (#E98F00).
