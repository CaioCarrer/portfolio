Urban Intervention and Outreach

The flyposters were individually developed for the Project 1 course within the Graphic Design program at Unesp Bauru. The assignment was to create a type of urban intervention that showcased the "Mutirão Bem Viver" and its worldview. The posters were designed to evoke a sense of strangeness and capture the attention of pedestrians through a headline, leading the reader to the second part of the flyposter: an invitation to visit the Bem Viver's linktr.ee webpage.

The chosen photographs relate to the theme of each poster, with the fire alluding to the destruction caused by agribusiness, and the bus of the city where the intervention would take place (Bauru).
The selected font was Ginora Sans Bold, a strong, easy-to-read sans-serif font; and some words were highlighted with a different size and the color #11C92B to attract more attention.
